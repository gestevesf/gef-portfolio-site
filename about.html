Hello, I am...

Gonçalo Ferreira, a UX designer from Portugal living in Berlin.
I was a Pastry Chef / Cook for 7 years, until transitioning to the UX Design field. I’ve studied in CareerFoundry, making a specialization in Frontend in 2021.
My goal is to solve problems using Human Centered Design principles, designing accessible digital products, providing a pleasing and satisfying experiences for.

I’m a very hard worker, creative, a team player, I easely adapt to any environment or situation and I’m never afraid to express my feelings and ideas at any moment.

How working in a kitchen made me a better designer?

Working everyday in a kitchen made me ready to manage and deal with the unexpected. It improved my attention to detail, made me a better team player under intense conditions and allowed me to think out of the box to create different and new solutions. I believe coming up with new recipies developed and fostered my creativity, that I now apply on my projects.

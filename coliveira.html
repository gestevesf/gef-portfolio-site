Overview

Problem

Carlos’ website was outdated and difficult to navigate, resulting in a decrease in customer engagement and inquiries.

Solution

The website was redesigned with an emphasis on improving aesthetics and organization, making it easier for potential customers to find relevant information quickly.

Duration

January 2023 - March 2023

Analysis

Outdated Website

The first step was to conduct a thorough analysis of the existing website to identify areas for improvement:

  1. The website was cluttered, with too many random photos and outdated graphics.
  2. Navigation was confusing, and important projects were buried deep in the site, making it difficult for potential customers to find what they were looking for.

Improvements

Aesthetics

To address these issues, I focused on improving the aesthetics of the site, with a cleaner, more modern design that emphasized clear, concise messaging. I also streamlined the navigation, making it easier for customers to find the information they needed on a big screen or smaller screen.

New Features

One key change was the addition of a Projects page that showcased Carlos' recent work.

I organized the projects by category, making it easy for potential customers to find examples of work that were relevant to their needs.

I also included high-quality images and brief descriptions of each project to give customers a clear sense of Carlos’ capabilities.

Results

Traffic

The redesign resulted in a significant increase in website traffic, with more customers staying on the site longer and exploring multiple pages. The addition of the Projects page, in particular, proved popular, with many potential customers spending significant time browsing the different categories and examples of work.

Increased Contacts

The changes also made it easier for customers to contact Carlos. I added a contact button to the navigation bar of the site, making it simple for customers to get in touch with questions or to request a quote. As a result, the client reported a notable increase in inquiries and new business as a direct result of the website redesign.

Final Thoughts

Overall

The redesign was a success, improving both the aesthetics and functionality of the site and resulting in a growth in customers seeing the website and making for quicker contacts.

Visit Card

New Modern Look

As a bonus work, I have also designed and visit card for Carlos. As most of his encounters with customers and possible clients are made through his ateliers or through spontaneous conversions, having a professional and updated card was important.

As so, I designed the card taking into consideration his new website, for similarities, and that most people don’t actually keep visit cards in their wallets anymore. For that reason besides the normal contact information I added a QR-Code for faster actions through smartphones.

Results

This addition, has since increased the contacts of customers and clients that have met him once, to quotes and new projects.
